Smart Matching
Your gap is
their skill.

Most platforms match on common interests. STACKD matches on complementary gaps — what you can teach vs what they need to learn, and a shared project to make it real.

  • Bidirectional matching — you teach React, they teach Figma. Both win.
  • Project-first pairing — matched around what you're building, not just who you are
  • Time-boxed collabs — 2 to 6 week sprints, not vague open-ended "partnerships"
  • No money, ever — knowledge is the only currency on STACKD
